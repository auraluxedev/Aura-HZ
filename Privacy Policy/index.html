Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Aura HZ mobile application (the "App") developed by Auraluxe Dev. This policy describes how we handle your data and is intended to inform you about our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

By downloading and using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage (Google Play Data Safety)

We are committed to a "Privacy by Design" philosophy. To be transparent with our users regarding the Google Play Data Safety section:

A. Personal Data

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This includes:

  • Names, Addresses, or Phone Numbers
  • Email Addresses
  • User IDs or Account Credentials
  • Financial or Payment Information

B. Device & Usage Data

The App operates as a standalone utility. We do not collect:

  • Device Identifiers (IMEI, MAC Address, Advertising ID)
  • Location Data (GPS or Coarse Location)
  • App Usage Statistics or Crash Logs (No third-party analytics)
  • Contacts or Calendar information

2. Internet & Network Access

The App functions primarily as an offline utility. However, the App requires internet permissions for specific, non-data-collecting system functions:

  • Google Play Licensing Service: To verify that the App was legitimately purchased/downloaded from the Google Play Store to prevent piracy.
  • Security Checks: To ensure the integrity of the APK file has not been tampered with.

No user content or personal data is transmitted during these verification processes.

3. Local Data Storage

All user-generated content and preferences are stored locally on your device within the App's isolated sandbox storage (`SQLite Database` and `SharedPreferences`).

This data includes:

  • Custom frequency presets
  • App settings (Volume, Haptics, Theme)
  • Usage streaks and gamification stats
  • Local alarm schedules

Note: Since we do not have cloud servers, uninstalling the App or clearing its data via Android Settings will permanently delete this local data. We cannot recover it for you.

4. Android Permissions Declaration

We request the following permissions solely to enable specific App functionalities:

A. POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)

Purpose: Used to display media playback controls in the notification shade and to deliver user-scheduled daily reminders.

B. VIBRATE & HAPTIC FEEDBACK

Purpose: Used for the "Breath Sync" feature to provide tactile feedback during meditation exercises.

C. AUDIO FOCUS & FOREGROUND SERVICE

Purpose: Required to play audio in the background while the screen is off or while using other apps. This ensures uninterrupted playback sessions.

D. SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM

Purpose: Used strictly for the "Daily Reminder" feature to trigger notifications at the exact time chosen by the user.
