Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Aura HZ mobile application (the "App") developed by Auraluxe Dev. This policy explains how we handle your data and outlines our commitment to your privacy.

By downloading, installing, and using the App, you explicitly agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

1. Data Collection & Usage (Google Play Data Safety)

Aura HZ is built upon a strict "Privacy by Design" architecture. To provide total transparency regarding the Google Play Data Safety requirements:

A. Personal Data

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Names, Physical Addresses, or Phone Numbers
  • Email Addresses
  • User IDs, Account Credentials, or Passwords
  • Financial or Payment Information

B. Device & Usage Data

The App operates as a self-contained, offline-first utility. We do not collect:

  • Device Identifiers (IMEI, MAC Address, Android ID, or Advertising ID)
  • Location Data (GPS, Wi-Fi, or Coarse Location)
  • App Usage Statistics or Crash Logs (No third-party telemetry)
  • Contacts, Calendars, or Media Files outside the App's scope

2. Internet & Network Access

While Aura HZ functions primarily offline, it requests network access permissions for critical, non-data-collecting security functions:

  • Anti-Piracy & Integrity Checks: To verify the cryptographic signature of the App and ensure it was legitimately installed via the Google Play Store or authorized channels.
  • Security Validation: To perform background checks that protect the App from unauthorized tampering, cloning, or reverse engineering.

Zero user content, preferences, or personal data is transmitted during these security verifications.

3. Local Data Storage & Encryption

All user-generated content and configurations are stored locally on your device within the App's isolated and encrypted sandbox (`Room Database` and `EncryptedSharedPreferences`).

This local data includes:

  • Custom binaural frequency presets
  • Application settings (Volume, Haptics, Theme)
  • Usage streaks and session statistics
  • Local alarm and timer schedules

Important: Because we do not use cloud servers, uninstalling the App or clearing its storage via Android Settings will permanently and irreversibly delete this data. We cannot recover it for you.

4. Android Permissions Declaration

We request the following device permissions solely to enable core App functionalities:

A. POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)

Purpose: Required to display media playback controls in the notification shade and to deliver user-scheduled daily reminders.

B. VIBRATE

Purpose: Used to provide tactile haptic feedback during meditation exercises and UI interactions.

C. FOREGROUND_SERVICE & MEDIA_PLAYBACK

Purpose: Strictly required by the Android OS to generate and play high-fidelity audio frequencies in the background while the device screen is off or while using other applications.

D. SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM

Purpose: Utilized exclusively for the "Daily Reminder" feature to trigger precise local notifications at the time chosen by the user.
